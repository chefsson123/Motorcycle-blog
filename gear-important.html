How important is gear

ATGATT - All the gear, all the time.

Imagine walking through a building that is on fire. You can choose being in a t-shirt and shorts or in a fully fire-resistant suit, what would you pick? Think of fire as all the danger surrounding you on your ride.

Even a casual ride comes with risks, so you better be prepared.

No matter how experienced you are in motorcycling, there are inevitably times, in which you do not feel like gearing up. It Could be a simple ride to the market, a visit to a friend who lives next corner, any kind of low-range ride that feels like it does not require gearing up.

In fact, a huge amount of accidents occur when riding nearby. This is the time when we are not 100% focused and may find ourselves on the ground. Hell, you can even fall down because you forgot the sidestand. It may sound stupid, but it happens all the time.

It is totally up to you whether you gear up or not, but we at MotoLand have some mandatory rules of our own. One of them is - never ride without at least having a helmet and a pair of gloves. These are our top picks when we're talking gear. Overall, it may sound a bit disturbing, wearing gear is the difference between a few minor scratches and bearing with your wounds for the rest of your life.

Always gear up, preparing for the unknown.
